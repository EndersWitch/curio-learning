Overview
What to expect in Grade 4

Afrikaans First Additional Language in Grade 4 introduces learners to Afrikaans as a practical communication tool. The CAPS curriculum focuses on building functional literacy in Afrikaans — reading simple texts with understanding, writing short sentences and paragraphs, and using basic grammatical structures. Learners develop core Afrikaans vocabulary, simple grammar and reading skills through everyday contexts. By the end of Grade 4, learners should be able to write a short paragraph in Afrikaans, answer basic comprehension questions and hold a simple conversation.

CAPS curriculum
Term-by-term breakdown
Term 1
Introduction & simple Afrikaans texts
  • Basic Afrikaans vocabulary — greetings, family, colours, numbers (een tot tien)
  • Simple narrative texts — read and answer literal questions in Afrikaans
  • Common nouns (soortnaamwoorde) — people, animals, things
  • Present tense of common Afrikaans verbs
  • Write short Afrikaans sentences about yourself and your family
  • Oral: basic Afrikaans greetings and classroom conversations
Term 2
Description & vocabulary building
  • Read descriptive Afrikaans texts and identify key information
  • Adjectives (byvoeglike naamwoorde) — colour, size, shape
  • Singular and plural of simple Afrikaans nouns
  • Punctuation: full stop (punt), question mark (vraagteken)
  • Write a short description of a picture or place in Afrikaans
  • Oral: describe a picture using simple Afrikaans sentences
Term 3
Informational texts & past tense
  • Read simple Afrikaans informational texts — notices, instructions
  • Simple past tense of regular Afrikaans verbs
  • Synonyms (sinonieme) — expand vocabulary
  • Prepared reading with focus on Afrikaans pronunciation
  • Write a short report about an activity or event in Afrikaans
  • Oral: tell about a past experience using Afrikaans past tense
Term 4
Revision & poetry
  • Simple persuasive and poetic Afrikaans texts — identify main idea
  • Rhyme (rym) and rhythm (ritme) in simple Afrikaans poems
  • Revision of nouns, verbs and adjectives in Afrikaans
  • Revision of present and past tense in Afrikaans
  • Write a simple short Afrikaans poem or rhyme
  • Oral: recite a memorised Afrikaans poem or rhyme

Tips for Grade 4
📖

Bou woordeskat daagliks. Leer elke dag 5 nuwe Afrikaanse woorde. Skryf dit neer met 'n Engelse vertaling en gebruik dit in 'n sin. Binne 'n maand het jy 150 nuwe woorde geleer.

✏️

Gebruik woordeboek-klankborde. Wanneer jy nie seker is oor uitspraak nie, lees die woord hardop. Afrikaans word byna presies gespel soos dit klink.

📝

Vir begripsopgawes — soek die antwoord in die teks. Die meeste antwoorde op letterlike vrae is direk in die gegewe teks. Leer om die teks te skandeer vir sleutelwoorde.

🌱

Moenie bang wees om foute te maak nie. Die beste manier om Afrikaans te leer is om dit te gebruik — selfs as dit nie perfek is nie. Elke fout is 'n kans om te leer.
