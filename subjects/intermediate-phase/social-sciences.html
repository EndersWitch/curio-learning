Tips for Social Sciences
🗺️

Know your maps inside out. Practice drawing and labelling SA's provinces, major cities and physical features. Map questions appear in almost every paper.

📅

Use timelines to sequence history. Drawing your own timeline of key events forces you to understand the order and connections — much better than just reading notes.

💬

Practise source-based questions. History questions often give you an image or quote and ask "What does this tell us?" Always refer back to the source in your answer.

🌿

Connect Geography to current events. Climate change, water shortages, urbanisation — these are all in the news AND in your syllabus. Reading widely makes your answers richer.
