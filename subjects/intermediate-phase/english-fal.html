Tips for English FAL
🗣️

Speak English as often as you can. FAL is about building communication — the more you use English in daily life, even in short conversations, the faster your confidence and fluency grow.

📖

Read simple English texts every day. Children's books, short news articles, comic strips — anything that is slightly above your comfort level will stretch your vocabulary and comprehension.

🔤

Keep a vocabulary notebook. Every new English word you learn — write it down with its meaning and a sentence. Review 10 words every night. Small daily habits make a massive difference over a term.

✏️

Don't worry about perfection — practise writing anyway. FAL examiners want to see effort and basic correctness. Short, clear sentences are better than long, tangled ones. Write daily, even if it's just one paragraph.
