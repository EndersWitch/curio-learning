Tips for Life Skills
💭

Connect theory to your own experience. Life Skills questions often ask you to explain or give examples from real life. The more personal and specific your answer, the better.

📖

Know your definitions. Terms like "self-concept", "Ubuntu", "balanced diet" and "peer pressure" need precise definitions — learn them and practise writing them out.

🎨

For Creative Arts: understand the elements. In the written component, you need to describe and analyse — know the elements of art (line, colour, shape, texture) and music (rhythm, melody, beat).

🤝

Use scenarios to practise. Life Skills often presents a situation ("Sipho is being bullied…") and asks what you would do. Practise writing structured responses: identify the problem, explain your action, give a reason.
