Tips for Afrikaans FAL
🗣️

Listen to Afrikaans often. Radio stations, songs or TV shows in Afrikaans train your ear and help vocabulary stick much faster than memorising word lists.

📚

Learn verb tenses properly. The difference between teenwoordige, verlede and toekomende tyd is tested every year. Practise with short sentences daily.

✍️

Write a short paragraph every week. Start with describing your room, your day or your pet. Keep it simple — grammar accuracy matters more than big words.

🔤

Build your woordeskat deliberately. When you encounter a new Afrikaans word, write it down with its meaning and use it in a sentence. Ten new words a week compounds quickly.
