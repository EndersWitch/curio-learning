Tips for English HL
📖

Read every day. Even 15 minutes of reading builds vocabulary and comprehension faster than any other method. Read anything — stories, news, cereal boxes.

✏️

Learn your parts of speech. Nouns, verbs, adjectives and adverbs come up in almost every paper. Make flashcards and test yourself until they're automatic.

📝

Practice writing with a time limit. In exams you have set time — practice at home so you're comfortable planning and writing quickly and clearly.

🔍

For comprehension, always quote the text. When asked to give evidence, copy a phrase from the passage. It shows the examiner you read carefully.
