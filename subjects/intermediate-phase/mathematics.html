Tips for Mathematics
🔢

Know your times tables cold. Up to 12×12 should be instant recall. Everything — fractions, division, algebra later — depends on multiplication fluency.

📐

Always show your working. In Maths exams you earn marks for method — even if your final answer is wrong, showing steps gets you partial marks.

🔄

Check answers by working backwards. After solving, plug your answer back into the question to see if it works. This habit catches most errors before the exam.

📊

Draw diagrams for geometry. Sketch the shape, label it, then work from the drawing. Visual problems become much easier when you can see them.
