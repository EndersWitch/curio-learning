Tips for Natural Sciences & Technology
🔬

Draw and label everything. Diagrams of life cycles, food chains, circuits and body systems are almost always in exams. Practising the drawings cements the concepts.

📋

Learn the vocabulary precisely. NS&T has specific scientific terms — "photosynthesis", "conductor", "habitat". Examiners use these words exactly, and so should you.

💡

Connect concepts to real life. Why do animals migrate? Why does ice melt? When you link theory to things you've seen, answers come naturally in exams.

🌍

Understand the technology strand. The design process — identify, design, make, evaluate — comes up as structured questions. Know each step and what it means.
